esk.ph Registration Policy

Public or Private educational institutions operating in the Philippines may register under the ESK.ph domain. Primary and secondary institutions have to be recognized by the Department of Education (DepEd). Tertiary institutions have to be recognized by the Commission on Higher Education (CHED). State educational institutions have to be recognized by the state through an appropriate Republic Act. Other educational/training institutions have to be recognized by TESDA.

Any authorized representative of an educational institution may register a domain in behalf of the institution.

-> schoolname.esk.ph domain name & G Suite for Education setup with administration

A G Suite for Education setup for your school includes school-wide GMail services, Google Classroom and Video/Voice Conferencing.
