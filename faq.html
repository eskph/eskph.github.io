ESK.ph FAQ:

Who can register an ESK.ph domain?

Public or Private educational and training institutions operating in the Philippines can register under the ESK.ph domain (schoolname.esk.ph). Primary and secondary institutions have to be recognized by the Department of Education. Tertiary institutions have to be recognized by the CHED. State educational institutions have to be recognized by the state. Training institutions have to be recognized by TESDA.

Why is it free?

We don't charge for the registration of a schoolname.esk.ph domain name or the setup & administration of G Suite for Education, because we would like schools, it's students, faculty and staff to have a formal point of contact for the purpose of security and privacy when using online collaboration platforms.
